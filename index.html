Our Core Services

Comprehensive electrical and automation solutions tailored to your requirements.

Solar Energy

Complete solar power solutions — N-Type Monocrystalline panels, LiFePO4 battery storage, smart hybrid inverters, and off-grid/on-grid system design and installation.

Smart Automation

Home and building automation, IoT integration, smart lighting, climate control, and voice-activated systems for modern living.

Security Solutions

Advanced surveillance, biometric access control, electric fencing, alarm systems, and integrated security management.

ICT Infrastructure

Regular maintenance, troubleshooting, upgrades, and support for all installed electrical and automation systems.
